XFBT: Fractional Burn Token

Deflationary Burn Token that determines inflation rate of all other assets based on its activity.

Initial Supply: 10.9 billion*
Decimals: 18

(*:1/person at projected peak pop.)

XFRS: Fractional Reserve Share

Reserve Shares are an indivisible asset representing a unit of the underlying pool of assets (XFBT, XFF, XFN).

Initial Supply: 0
Decimals: 0

XFF: Fractional Fiat

Inflationary asset intended to be more liquid than other asset classes with fine tunable inflation control.

Initial Supply: 1 trillion
Decimals: 6

XFN: Fractional Entropy

Scarce deflationary asset that gets exponentially more scarce as core tax rate is lowered.

Initial Supply: 0
Decimals: 18

DAO Token (Future)

To ensure this new money supply remains decentralized, it must have and be governed by a DAO.

Current Team Size: 1
Necessary Unfilled Roles: 7

    Core Tax Fractions

  • taxFraction: 10, for 1/10
  • brintFraction: 2, for 1/2
  • opsFraction: 2, for 1/2
  • fiatFraction: 100, for 100/1
  • entropyFraction: 5, for 1/5

    Reserve Pool Fractions

  • XFBT:2, for 1/2 of tax value (remainder burnt)
  • XFF:3, for 1/3 of brinted XFF
  • XFN:2, for 1/2 of brinted XFN

All core tax and reserve fractions can be tweaked by the DAO (once organized).

taxFraction hardcoded to >= 7 for divisor (14.2857% max core tax).

Read the Litepaper to learn more about the tinting, brinting & reserve process under the hood.
